About Our School

Excellence in Education,
Rooted in Community

Lyceo de Pasacao, Inc. is a trusted private school in Sta. Rosa Del Norte, Pasacao, Camarines Sur. Under the leadership of School Administrator Mary Ann O. Seva, we deliver holistic education aligned with the Department of Education's K–12 standards.

As a recognized DepEd institution in Region V – Bicol Region, we serve Pasacao and surrounding communities with dedicated educators and a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for life beyond the classroom.

Academic Excellence
Rigorous, DepEd-aligned curriculum building a strong foundation for every student.
Character Formation
Instilling integrity, respect, and responsibility as the cornerstone of student life.
Community Engagement
Building strong ties with families and the broader Pasacao community we serve.
Holistic Growth
Nurturing intellectual, physical, social, and spiritual development of every learner.

Enrollment

How to Join Our Family

Follow these simple steps to enroll your child at Lyceo de Pasacao, Inc.

Enrollment Process

  • 1
    Visit the School Office
    Come to Sta. Rosa Del Norte, Pasacao, Cam. Sur during office hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
  • 2
    Submit Requirements
    Prepare and submit all required documents. Our admissions staff will guide you through the process.
  • 3
    Assessment & Orientation
    Students may be assessed for grade level placement. A brief parent and student orientation may be conducted.
  • 4
    Pay Enrollment Fees
    Settle applicable fees. Payment schedules and options are available — inquire at the school office.
  • 5
    Welcome to Lyceo!
    Receive your schedule, school ID, and materials. You're now part of the Lyceo family!
📋 Documents Required
  • PSA Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Report Card / Form 138 from previous school
  • Good Moral Certificate (for transferees)
  • Certificate of Completion or Diploma
  • 2×2 ID Photos — at least 2 copies
  • Barangay Certificate of Residency
  • Medical Certificate or Health Record
  • Form 137 / School Records (for transferees)

Have questions about enrollment? Our admissions office is happy to assist. Call or visit us during office hours.
